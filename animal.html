ABOUT US

AKRON,BARBERTON & WADSWORTH

Our practices are geared to meet the needs of any and all of your family pets. With the Barberton Veterinary Clinic, the Animal Clinic-Wadsworth and the emergency service at the Metropolitan Veterinary Hospital in Copley, we offer our patients 24 hour, 7 day a week care. We love ALL pets! We offer state-of-the-art care for dogs, cats, rabbits, ferrets, pocket pets, reptiles, birds, and MORE. Dr. Riggs is an ABVP specialist in avian practice and all of our staff are highly experienced in the treatment of the entire spectrum of pet species. Our patients come in all shapes and sizes but our #1 goal remains the same for all…long-term health and vitality. We’ll work with you to keep your pets happy and healthy!
